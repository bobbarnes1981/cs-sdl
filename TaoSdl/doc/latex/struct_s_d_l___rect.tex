\subsection{Detailed Description}
Defines a rectangular area. A \hyperlink{struct_s_d_l___rect}{SDL\_\-Rect} defines a rectangular area of pixels. It is used by \hyperlink{_sdl_8cs_aec41f77b04d717457433acbecd7a257b}{SDL\_\-BlitSurface} to define blitting regions and by several other video functions.
